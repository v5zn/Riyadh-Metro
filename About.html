About Riyadh Metro

The Riyadh Metro is a massive public transportation project aimed at improving traffic flow in the Saudi capital, providing a modern train network covering most parts of the city. The metro contributes to a smooth and comfortable commuting experience for Riyadh residents and visitors, and promotes the city's sustainable development. This project is considered one of the largest public transportation projects in the region.

Capacity

The Metro Project is the backbone of the public transport network in Riyadh, capable of transporting 3.6 million passengers per day.

Safety

Each train and station are equipped with advanced surveillance systems, firefighting equipment, tunnel safety systems, and sophisticated communications between the network and the Operations & Control Center. The trains have been subjected to intense testing to ensure they can withstand the city’s harsh climatic conditions.

Riyadh: On the Rails of Progress

The Riyadh Metro is a significant addition to the Saudi capital, impacting various aspects of city life. This massive project is more than just a transportation system; it's an investment in the city's future. By reducing traffic congestion and improving air quality, the metro contributes to a healthier and more sustainable environment. Furthermore, it provides a fast and convenient mode of transportation, boosting individual productivity and stimulating economic growth. Additionally, the metro plays a crucial role in developing areas around stations, encouraging the establishment of new commercial and residential projects, thus revitalizing economic activity and creating new job opportunities.
